What is Excel Comp

Excel Comp is a tool that helps you to compare two excel documents or two excel sheets in one file online. It highlights all differences between the two files so that you can find changes quickly. It also allows you find and remove duplicates and blank cells and even merge the two files into one

What Types of Documents Can I Compare?

Using our online platform, you can compare excel documents side-by-side! the documents have to be in common excel file formats like xls, xlsx and csv.

Will I have to pay to use Excel Comp?

How do I compare two excel files

    After signing into your account
  • Upload the two files and wait for the upload process to finish
  • Select what other criteria you want to highlight in the two files asides from the differences
  • Select the specific sheets in which the data you want to compare is contained in
  • Then click on compare

How do I compare sheets within one excel file

    After signing into your account
  • Upload the excel files twice and wait for the upload process to finish
  • Select what other criteria you want to highlight in the two files asides from the differences
  • Select the specific sheets in which the data you want to compare is contained in
  • Then click on compare
